HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to full cylinders.

No memberships, no waiting. Just show up, swop, and go.

1
Find your nearest station
Use the GAZ2GO app or the Find gas near me button on our home page to locate the closest exchange station — available at high-traffic retail sites near you.
2
Bring your empty 9kg cylinder
Arrive with your empty LPG cylinder. No need to book ahead — our stations are open 24/7, every day of the year.
3
Pay, Swop, Done
Pay via card, load your empty cylinder and retrieve a full cylinder. The whole process takes under two minutes.
